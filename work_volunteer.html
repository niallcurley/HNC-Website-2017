Work/Volunteer


Work or Volunteer With Us

Get valuable experience working with our youth & children. Seeing them grow and develop in confidence each week can be very rewarding. Be part of the team that makes dreams a reality, help mould our youth, into the stars of the future.We are always looking for new volunteers for each new term.



Positions Available

Dance instructor - we are looking for a dance instructor, this person needs to be someone who is passionate and dedicated to teaching young people how to express themselves through dance - this is part time role. Salary to be negotiated (apply below).

Drama Volunteer Assistant -this voluntary role will involve supervising and working with youth and children during main drama sessions. Requires a fun person who is interested in inspiring children beteween the ages of 6-12 years of age (apply below).


Get Accreditation -

At Icons Drama we give accreditation to employees and volunteers for drama youth work. As well as the fulfilling sense that you get from helping young people develop, Icons Drama awards accreditation to any person has been with us for two terms (20 weeks). This can be useful for those seeking employment in similar work, like teaching or other youth work. Some of the feedback from these volunteers over the years has been encouraging; Emily Rice, 'I volunteered for 20 weeks with icons and it really opened my eyes to what can be acheived with children through playful encouragement'.


St Mary Teacher Training -

We have over the years given experience to aspiring teachers who attend St Marys teacher training Colledge. Experience working with youth and children is essential for those seeking entrance unto the PGCE qualification in Coleraine. If you are interested in gaining experience through our 20 week qualification, please contact us.
