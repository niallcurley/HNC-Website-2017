Disability Friendly

Everyone Welcome!!

At Icons Drama we love to work with children of all abilities. Our belief is that every child is an important part of our community and we work with them to bring out hidden potential.

Autism Awareness

Click

Icons drama is an organisation that is 'Autism Aware'. We seek to integrate all our children on the spectrum at every level. Recent studies have shown that drama helps children within the austic spectrum develop their communication and inter personal skills.

Mencap

Click

We work in partnership with Mencap and other disability organisations to bring together children that have disabilites with those that have none.Our family ethos creates a sense of openness which all our children thrive in. In all our classes all our children develop together breaking down ignorance about disabilities. All our staff have carried out training through the Volunteer Development Agency and are sensitive to the needs of children with disabilites.
