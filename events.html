Upcoming Events

Nativity - (16th, 17th, December 2016)
This Christmas Special will be shown in St Agnes Parish Centre and will include some our brightest talents. Mary and Joseph (Rachael Reid and Niall Kearney) are a young married couple who recieve an unexpected messenger (Patrick Britton). This causes lots of talk in their village. Watch as Mary and Joseph travel to Bethlehem and the wicked king Herod (Josh Allen), trys to snuff out the true King.


Annie (22nd, 27th January 2017)

This hit musical about the red haired orphan Annie (Zoey Hamilton) and her dreams to be adopted by a loving family is full of comedy, drama and singing. Miss Hannigan (Christine Smith) plots to try and prevent this. It will be shown at St Michaels Parish Hall.


Jack And The Beanstalk (10th, 12th April 2017)

Our Easter Special is the tale of a young boy Jack (John Gibson) who scales the heights of a magic beanstalk. His adventures take him to a whole new country where he finds his fortune.


Recent Events

Cinderella (October 2016)

This colourful production was a real success, our youth choir was has been much applauded in the Belfast Telegraph & Irish News.Cinderella played by (Zoey Hamilton) was vocally sensational aswell. Her step sisters played by (Christine Smith & Shannon Hall) were completely self centred and ridicolously funny.


The Wizard Of Oz (May 2016)

We followed the yellow brick road with Dorothy (Julie Hobson) and it proved to be real show peice. It was a singing foursome with lions, tigers and bears. The cowardly lion (Patrick Britton) was comical and the dopey scarecrow (Josh Allen) was scatty, and the tin man (Niall Kearny) was so sentimental that we were all in tears. The Andersontown News did a special peice on this successful event.
