FAQ - Any Questions

Q.1 What is the earliest age that Icons take in students?

The earliest age is six years, but if your child will be turning six during the term they can apply.



Q.2 How much does it cost?

Each class is £2.50, there are two classes per week - thats a total is £5.00 per week (£50.00 per term). However there is a slight discount if you pay online £45.00 (see register).


Q.3 How long is each term & how many terms are there each year?

Each term is 10 weeks, and there are four terms per year and an optional one during the summer months.


Q.4 When does your terms run?

The first term, (January - February), second term, (March - April), third term, (May - June), fourth term (Late September - Late November), there is a 4 week optional course in July.


Q.5 How long is each class?

Each class is 90min.


Q.5 Is there any free trials?

Yes we have an open week at the start of January.


Q.6 What sort of clothes do children need?

When you sign up to the first term a free t-shirt is provided.


Q.7 How do you find out about working or volunteering with icons?

Check out our work/volunteer link on the orange side bar, you can see if icons are looking for people at the moment.


Q.8 How can I contact icons?

Check out our 'Contact Us' page in the top navigation bar, all our contact details are there, and a query form can be filled in, so we will get back to you.
