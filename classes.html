Classes & Information

Term Fees

Term Fees are £50 for (10 weeks) - this can be paid at £2.50 per class or by one single payment of £45.00 through our register payment option (see 'Register').


Tots (6-8 yrs)

These classes are held on Mondays and Wednesdays at 4pm - 6pm.
During these classes, the juniors are involved in a variety of activities, such as singing, dancing and drama. Juniors get to develop their skills from a young age and engage in child-friendly acitivies and get to make new friends!


Juniors (9-12 yrs)

These classes are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 6pm - 8pm.
This group focuses on developing skills which had been previously taught in Tots, as well as developing new skills for those new to Icons. This group allows individuals to focus on growing their imagination, creating their own characters and using their dramatic skills. This will allow the children to focus on character-growth skills and expand their confidence.


Seniors (13-16 yrs)

These classes are held on Saturdays and Mondays at 7pm - 9pm.
Seniors works with teenagers, and focuses on team building, as well as confidence growth. This follows on from lessons learnt in the Juniors class, and uses those skills to make their own productions. This group will identify each individuals strengths, talents and abilities and uses them to help each individual be the best that they can be. This also focuses on leadership skills.
